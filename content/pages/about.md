## About Nfinnerty Electrical

Nfinnerty Electrical is a family-run electrical contracting business serving homeowners, landlords and businesses across York, North Yorkshire and the surrounding area.

Founded on the belief that quality workmanship and honest pricing go hand in hand, we've built a reputation for reliability, professionalism and attention to detail that our customers can depend on.

---

### Our Story

We started as a small operation focused on delivering great electrical work without the drama — no inflated call-out fees, no unexpected costs and no corner-cutting. Word spread, and we've grown steadily thanks to the loyalty of our customers and the referrals they send our way.

Today, our team covers a wide area across North Yorkshire, handling everything from straightforward socket additions to full rewires and commercial electrical installations.

---

### Our Qualifications

Every job we carry out meets the current British Standard BS 7671 (IET Wiring Regulations). Our credentials include:

- **NICEIC Approved Contractor** — the UK's leading electrical safety register
- **Part P Certified** — all notifiable domestic electrical work is properly certified
- **Public Liability Insurance** — full cover for your peace of mind
- **EV Charger Installer** — OZEV registered for government-backed EV charge point installations

---

### Our Approach

We believe in clear communication from the very first call. Before any work begins, we'll give you a straightforward quote with no hidden extras. On the day, we arrive when we say we will, treat your property with care and leave the job site clean and tidy.

Every installation and repair is tested and, where required, a completion certificate or inspection report is issued so you have the documentation you need.

### Areas We Cover

We're based in York and cover the whole of the surrounding region, including Harrogate, Selby, Wetherby, Knaresborough, Tadcaster, Pocklington, Easingwold and many more towns and villages across North Yorkshire and East Yorkshire.
