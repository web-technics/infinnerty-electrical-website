## Commercial Electrician Services

Nfinnerty Electrical provides reliable commercial electrical services for businesses, landlords and letting agents. Our qualified, NICEIC-approved electricians deliver safe, certified electrical work for a wide range of commercial and business properties.

Whether you need a full electrical installation, rewiring, emergency call-out or a routine inspection, our team is ready to help.

---

### Commercial Electrical Services We Offer

- **Commercial Rewiring** — Full and partial rewires for offices, retail units, warehouses and other business premises
- **Three-Phase Supply Installation** — Supply upgrades for commercial and industrial properties with high power demands
- **Commercial Lighting** — LED office lighting, warehouse lighting, external security lighting and emergency lighting systems
- **Consumer Unit & Distribution Board Upgrades** — Replacing ageing fuse boards and distribution boards to current regulations
- **EV Charger Installation (Commercial)** — Charging solutions for business car parks and commercial fleets
- **Electrical Installation Condition Reports (EICR)** — Periodic inspection and testing required for commercial premises and rental properties
- **Landlord Certificates** — EICR reports, tenant changeover certificates and compliance documentation for landlords
- **Emergency Call-Outs** — Fast response to electrical faults, tripped supplies and safety issues
- **Fault Finding & Repairs** — Professional diagnosis and resolution of electrical problems in commercial settings
- **Outdoor & Security Electrics** — External power supplies, CCTV power, access control and security lighting
